Purple Crayon AI replaces all of it. One AI subscription. Your phone. Day one.

When Hal and his partner launched Urban Commercial Cleaning, LLC in Roanoke, Virginia, they were competing against companies with years of established systems, polished proposals, and professional documentation.

They had none of that. What they had was the ability to do the work — and the need to win the job first.

The startup costs that come before the first client — the contract templates, the quoting tools, the marketing, the admin overhead — were real barriers. Barriers that kill most startups before they find their footing.

So Hal built a system to eliminate them. He photographed walkthrough notes off a napkin in a parking lot and had a complete professional quote, contract, scope of work, and invoice ready before he started the engine. He named it HAL-AI — after himself — because that is exactly what it was. His own AI.

Urban Commercial Cleaning competed and won. Not because they had more experience. Because they responded faster and looked more professional than anyone expected from a brand new company.

Then other owners started asking about it. Lawn care. HVAC. Pressure washing. Pest control. Same problem. Different trade.

So Hal packaged the system, expanded it to every field service trade, and renamed it Purple Crayon AI — because the best tool is the simplest one. The one anyone can pick up and use on day one.

How It Works

One photo.
Everything you need.

From any scrap of information to a complete, client-ready package. No templates to fill in. No forms to complete. No tech skills required.

You are at a site visit. You took notes on whatever was nearby — a napkin, a notepad, the back of a flyer. You know the square footage, the rooms, the frequency they want, the contact name.

You photograph those notes. You open Purple Crayon on your phone. You type three words: Process this lead.

Purple Crayon reads your handwriting, applies your pricing matrix, benchmarks against local competitor rates, and generates the complete package — quote, contract, scope of work, job checklist, invoice, and a marketing post for when the job is done.

All of it branded to your business. All of it ready to send. Before you start the engine.

That is what Hal needed when Urban Commercial Cleaning launched. That is what Purple Crayon delivers — to every field service business, from day one.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is this software I need to install?+
No. Purple Crayon runs inside Claude AI — a free app on your phone. Nothing to install. If you can use a messaging app, you can use Purple Crayon today.
Does it really read napkin notes and handwriting?+
Yes. Claude's visual recognition reads handwritten notes reliably as long as the writing is reasonably legible and the photo is in focus. If something is unclear, it asks you to clarify rather than guess.
Does it work for my trade?+
If you quote jobs for a living, it works. Cleaning, lawn care, HVAC, pressure washing, pest control, painting, handyman, pool service, plumbing, roofing — all supported. The system is configured with your specific trade's pricing structure and document formats.
What does it cost to run?+
Your Purple Crayon package fee plus a Claude Pro subscription at $20/month billed directly by Anthropic. That is the only ongoing cost. No hidden fees, no per-document charges.
How long does setup take?+
About 60 minutes, once. Create a free Claude account, set up your Purple Crayon project, and fill in a configuration workbook with your rates, SOPs, and branding. You never configure it again unless your business changes.
I run more than one trade — does it cover both?+
Yes. Pro plan customers can configure multiple trade profiles. If you run a cleaning business and a pressure washing side hustle, both are set up and ready to go from the same account.
What if I'm just starting out and have no systems yet?+
That is exactly who Purple Crayon was built for. Hal built it when Urban Commercial Cleaning had no systems, no templates, and no budget for overhead. The Configuration Workbook walks you through building your pricing and SOPs as you set it up — you end the session with a fully operational business system.
